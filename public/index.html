About Me

As a student at Bath Spa University, I have been immersed in game development for some time, primarily through passion projects I've worked on during my studies. A lot of these started as fun experiments or uni assignments, but they've turned into something way more personal. I've also had the chance to work in teams, which has taught me a lot about collaboration and how cool it is to build something together from the ground up when given a chance. Outside of making games, I'm a huge anime fan, and a lot of what I create is inspired by the stories, art styles, and energy you find in anime, and I try to bring that same vibe into the games I make. This site is where I'm putting everything out there, all the good and the not-so-good projects.
